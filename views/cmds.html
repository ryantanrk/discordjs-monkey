Commands (Prefix: m!)

Go Back

  • help - Displays this link
  • about - Display everything about the bot
  • ping - Very straightforward
  • avatar - Gets the link to your avatar
  • say - Make the bot say whatever you want the bot to say

    Usage: say (arguments)

  • monkey - Get a random monkey gif
  • yesno - Make the monkey decide everything for you
  • count - Useless command that counts how many times it is used (for the session only, may reset whenever)
  • subcount - Shows the subscriber counts of channels that are associated with Edgy Kids (using YouTube API)
  • subbattle - Shows the current statistics of the PewDiePie vs T-Series sub race (using YouTube API)
  • math - Does some math.

    Usage: math (first number) (operand) (second number) [Make sure to add spaces in between]

  • upsidedown - Turns your sentence upside down.

    Usage: upsidedown (arguments)

  • bubble - Bubblifies your sentence.

    Usage: bubble (arguments)
